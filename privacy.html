Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to AlphaPrime Tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and tools.

2. What Data We Collect

For most tools (Word Counter, Password Generator, Text Converter, Base64 Tool, Image Tools):

  • No personal data collection: These tools work entirely in your browser
  • No file uploads: Your files and data never leave your device
  • No account required: We don't ask for emails, names, or personal information

For E-TRAX Expense Tracker:

  • Account information: Email address (for login and account recovery)
  • Financial data: Expense records you choose to save
  • Usage data: Basic analytics to improve the service

3. Browser-Based Processing

Most AlphaPrime Tools operate using local browser processing:

  • All computations happen on your device
  • Your text, images, and data stay on your computer
  • No server processing for most tools
  • No data transmission to our servers
  • Results are generated and displayed locally

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Essential Cookies:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Language settings
  • Tool preferences

Local Storage:

  • Temporary tool settings
  • Recently used data (only on your device)
  • For E-TRAX: Encrypted financial data

Analytics Cookies (if used):

  • Basic usage statistics
  • Page view counts
  • Tool popularity metrics
  • No personal identification

5. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our tools
  • To improve tool functionality and user experience
  • To understand how tools are used (aggregate data only)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent abuse

6. What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ We do NOT sell your personal data
  • ❌ We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • ❌ We do NOT track you across websites
  • ❌ We do NOT read your file contents
  • ❌ We do NOT require registration for most tools
  • ❌ We do NOT store your tool inputs on our servers (except E-TRAX data you choose to save)

7. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Website hosting
  • Analytics (anonymous data only)
  • Email delivery (for E-TRAX users)
  • Security monitoring

All third-party services are required to maintain confidentiality and security standards.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Secure data storage practices
  • Access controls for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits

9. Children's Privacy

AlphaPrime Tools is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
