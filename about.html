Our Smart Expense Tracker Implementation

  • check_circle Secure Authentication: Uses Google Firebase Auth so you can log in safely without us storing passwords.
  • check_circle Cloud Sync: Your expenses are stored in a secure cloud database, allowing access from your phone and laptop.
  • check_circle Data Ownership: You can export or delete your data at any time. We do not sell your financial data.
